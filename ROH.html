HIGHLIGHTS

    • Faust (2019)
    • 19 January-14 February 2022
    • Online only · Opera and music

    • Experience the decadence and elegance of David McVicar’s spectacular production of Gounod’s masterpiece, starring Michael Fabiano, Irina Lungu and Erwin Schrott. Watch on ROH Stream.

    • Romeo and Juliet
    • 29 January-26 February 2022
    • Main Stage · Ballet and dance

    • Shakespeare’s star-crossed lovers encounter passion and tragedy in Kenneth MacMillan’s 20th-century ballet masterpiece.

    • The Marriage of Figaro
    • 20-28 January 2022
    • Main Stage · Opera and music

    • Director David McVicar brings out the revolutionary elements in Mozart's great comic opera of intrigue, misunderstanding and forgiveness.

    • Nabucco (Live)
    • 21 January-20 February 2022
    • online only · Opera and music
    • Amartuvshin Enkhbat, Liudmyla Monastyrska and Alexander Vinogradov star in this powerful staging of Verdi’s epic opera. Watch on ROH Stream.
